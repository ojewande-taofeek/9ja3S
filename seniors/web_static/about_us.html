About us

Purpose

The Nigeria Senior Secondary School website, known as 9ja3S, aims to bridge the educational gap for out-of-school children and adults who wish to continue their
education at their own pace. Additionally, it serves as a valuable resource to support the efforts of school teachers and students in expanding their knowledge base.

Mission Statement

"To provide a comprehensive and accessible online learning platform that empowers out-of-school children and adults to continue their education at their own pace,
while also enhancing the educational resources available to school teachers and students."

Vision Statement

"To bridge the educational gap in Nigeria by becoming the leading online platform for lifelong learning, fostering an environment where all individuals have the
opportunity to achieve their educational goals and expand their knowledge base."
