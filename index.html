About this Project

Education shouldn't be for just the rich, it should be accessible to all. Unfortunately, this is not the case. With the continous increase in cost of education in the world, leading to surge in out-of-school children, it becomes a must to weaponise illiteracy. This is the role, the 9ja3S plays.

As a portfolio project for Holberton School, this application demonstrates the skills and technologies I have learned, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
